About us

Prague Regional Memorial Hospital is a proud partner of Cohesive Healthcare Management and Consulting. Through our relationship with Cohesive Healthcare, we are able to provide a level of care we’ve never been able to before. Established in 1952, Prague Regional Memorial Hospital in Prague, Oklahoma is a 25-bed critical access hospital. With more than 70 years of tradition and excellence, we provide quality care to patients every day for the communities of Lincoln, Pottawatomie, Okfuskee, and Seminole counties. We offer a wide range of state-of-the-art, high-quality medical services that allow us to improve the health of the community we serve. We have outpatient and inpatient radiology, CT, lab, and physical therapy. We offer the Strong Mind Program – an outpatient Geri psych program for those 55 and older. We also have inpatient dialysis. Our patients and community benefit from high nurse-to-patient ratios, 24-hour emergency services, connections to higher levels of care when needed, and close-to-home care 365 days a year.

Our Services

We, the physicians, staff, and board members of Prague Regional Memorial Hospital are committed to providing the highest level of healthcare possible at our facility.

Swing Bed

After surgery or hospitalization, some patients require additional care and transfer into a nursing home or rehabilitation facility to help improve their physical functioning.

Wound Care

Prague Regional Memorial Hospital offers high-quality care for slow healing and chronic wounds. We provide full assessment, tailored treatment, patient education and support.

Respiratory Therapy

Our respiratory therapists are essential members of the multidisciplinary healthcare team, collaborating with the physicians and nurses to provide compassionate, high-quality care to all areas of the hospital.

Rehabilitation

Prague Regional Memorial Hospital provides comprehensive Physical Therapy, Occupational Therapy and Sports Medicine services to help our patients return to an independent lifestyle.

Case Management & Social Services

Case Management and Social Services department is available to assist patients and their families as they cope with the many psychological and social problems that may arise during hospitalization and medical treatment.

Nursing

At Prague Regional Memorial Hospital our nursing staff prides itself on compassionate caregiving and nursing excellence.

Cohesive Renal Services Dialysis Program

At Cohesive Renal, our team of highly competent, seasoned professionals have many years of experience within the dialysis industry.

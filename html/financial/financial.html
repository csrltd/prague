Financial Services

In keeping with its mission, Prague Regional Memorial Hospital is dedicated to improving the quality of life in our community by providing excellent healthcare with compassion and respect. As part of that commitment, Prague Regional Memorial Hospital acknowledges the financial needs of patients and families who are unable to afford the charges associated with the cost of medical care and financial assistance to those who have an established need to receive medically necessary medical services.

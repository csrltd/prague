AnnDee Lee-Carter, PA-C

AnnDee grew up in Prague and is a graduate of Prague High School. She received her Master of Health Science degree in PA Studies from the University of Oklahoma, Tulsa, in December of 2016. She has been employed in Prague 3-1/2 years at Prague Clinic in Family Medicine as a PA-C in September of 2017. Highlighted Services

Physicals

Our physicals are a complete workup of each patient, ensuring they are fit for everyday activities and/or athletic exertion.

Women's Health

We offer a myriad of women’s health services, including annual checkups, pap smears, breast exams, and prescribing birth control.

Well-Child Check

We offer well-child checks to evaluate our littlest patients and ensure they’re on the right track to grow into healthy adults.

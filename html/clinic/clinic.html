AnnDee Lee-Carter

PA-C

AnnDee Lee-Carter graduated from Prague High School in 2009, then attended the University of Oklahoma where she graduated in the top 10% of her class. She received her Master in Health Science in Physician Assistant Studies in December of 2016 from the University of Oklahoma, Tulsa.

In 2018 Lee-Carter was asked to be part of the Preceptor Program. She jumped at the chance because she was interested in teaching. She was awarded The Preceptor of the by the University of Oklahoma – Tulsa in 2021. She truly loves teaching students and being a mentor to them.

In her spare time, she spends time with her son, Jaxsen. She also enjoys taking care of her flower and vegetable garden and likes to read.

Heather Brandwein

APRN-CNP

Heather Brandwein has worked as a Nurse Practitioner in Family Practice Primary Care and Inpatient/Swing bed services for the last 6 years. She has advanced training in wound care and provides wound care services to her clinic patients, as well as our inpatients. Prior to obtaining her master’s degree and Nurse Practitioner licensure, Heather worked as an RN at Saint Francis Hospital in multiple settings including Trauma/Emergency, Medical/Surgical and Kidney Transplant. She was raised in Morris, Oklahoma and is passionate about providing quality care to rural communities. Heather currently lives in Tulsa with her wife, daughter and their two golden retrievers. She is a lover of learning, cooking, basketball and spending time with her family.

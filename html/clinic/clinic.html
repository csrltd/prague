What we believe

AnnDee Lee-Carter, PA-C

AnnDee Lee-Carter graduated from Prague High School in 2009, then attended the University of Oklahoma where she graduated in the top 10% of her class. She received her Master in Health Science in Physician Assistant Studies in December of 2016 from the University of Oklahoma, Tulsa.

In 2018 Lee-Carter was asked to be part of the Preceptor Program. She jumped at the chance because she was interested in teaching. She was awarded The Preceptor of the by the University of Oklahoma – Tulsa in 2021. She truly loves teaching students and being a mentor to them.

In her spare time, she spends time with her son, Jaxsen. She also enjoys taking care of her flower and vegetable garden and likes to read.
